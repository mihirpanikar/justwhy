Who am I?

But before that, who are you?

Hello! I'm pretty sure I already helloed you on the homepage. But I figured I was worth repeating. You know, just in case you weren't paying attention or something. I am Mihir, just another curious piece of meat, standing on a rock that's travelling at about 9000 miles an hour around a gas giant in an infinite universe, which is a product of 13 billion years of cosmic evolution. Yeah, I'm that insignificant. And you are too. You should probably spend what little time there is doing something you like, instead of reading about a random stranger on the internet. Alright? Great. Have a great day, or night, if thats your thing.
